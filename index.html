How to Use the Radar

             
     
       

Welcome to the ICH Shared Heritage Radar. This tool maps and analyzes Intangible Cultural Heritage globally.

       
             
  • Map Nodes: Click on any pulsing green node on the map to see heritage elements from that region.
    •          
  • Connections: Dashed lines indicate a "Shared Heritage" relationship between two different countries or cultures.
    •          
  • Directory: Use the filters to sort by country or data completion status. Click "View Full Analysis" to read the AI-enriched data, tools, and processes.
